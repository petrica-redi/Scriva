01 — Brand Story

Voice becomes
documentation.

Scriva comes from the Latin scriba — the trusted scribe who recorded proceedings with precision and discretion. Across Europe the root resonates: scrivere (IT), escrever (PT), escribir (ES).

"The doctor speaks. Scriva writes."

We exist to give European clinicians their time back. Every consultation recorded, every clinical note generated — with AI that's GDPR-native, not US-first with EU compliance bolted on.

The mark combines three elements of the workflow: a document (the output), a microphone (the input), and a stethoscope disc (the medical context).

01b — Brand Values

Four principles.

Every design and product decision flows from these.

Trust

GDPR-native. EU data residency. Doctors trust us with their most sensitive data.

Clarity

No jargon. No clutter. Every screen understood in two seconds.

Precision

Medical-grade accuracy. Every word matters. Every transcript is verifiable.

European

Built for EU regulations, languages, and healthcare workflows from day one.

08 — EU Regulatory Positioning

Built GDPR-native.
Not retrofitted.

This is the core brand differentiator. Every US competitor bolted on EU compliance. Scriva was born compliant.

GDPR Native

Data protection by design and by default. Consent management, right to erasure, data portability — built in from day one.

EHDS Ready

Aligned with the European Health Data Space regulation (entered force March 2025). Primary and secondary use of health data.

EU Data Residency

All patient data processed and stored within EU borders. No transatlantic data transfers. No Schrems III risk.
