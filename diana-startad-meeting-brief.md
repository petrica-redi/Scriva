# 10. RISKS & WATCH-OUTS

- **Don't over-pitch.** This is a first call. The goal is: mutual interest → one concrete next step → in-person meeting in March. Not a signed contract.
- **Don't undersell either.** Diana's profile is genuinely exceptional for what they need. Own it.
- **Ashwin may see this as "nice to have" not urgent.** Counter by being specific: name their startups, name EU instruments, show you've done the homework.
- **They may have existing EU advisory relationships.** Ask: "How do your startups currently approach European markets?" — diagnostic, not assumptive.
- **Compensation model may not be obvious.** startAD is funded by Tamkeen/NYU — they have budgets for experts, but it may not be large retainers. Be flexible: start with value, monetize later. The real prize is the UAE positioning, not the startAD fees.
- **Don't mention relocation.** Ever. On this call, Diana is an EU-based expert with growing interest in the region. The relocation happens organically as engagement deepens.
